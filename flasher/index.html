BLOCK SYSTEM Hardware — flash the latest firmware from your browser. No drivers, no IDE, no setup. Calibration and settings on the device are preserved.
This browser can't talk to USB devices. Use Chrome, Edge or another Chromium-based browser on desktop, or follow the manual steps at the bottom of this page.

STEP 1 — ENTER UPDATE MODE

Plug the BitchBoy into this computer, then click below and pick BitchBoy from the list. The device will reboot into its bootloader (LEDs go dark — that's normal).
Already holding BOOTSEL / device shows up as a USB drive? Skip to step 2.

STEP 2 — FLASH

Click below and pick the RP2 Boot / RP2350 Boot device from the list. Flashing takes a few seconds. Don't unplug until it says done.

Manual fallback (no special browser needed)

1. Download the firmware: bitchboy-latest.uf2
2. Unplug the BitchBoy. Hold its BOOT button while plugging it back in — a USB drive named RP2350 (or RPI-RP2) appears.
3. Drag the .uf2 file onto that drive. The device reboots by itself.

Your slider/pot calibration and settings are preserved either way — they live in their own flash sector that firmware updates don't touch.
